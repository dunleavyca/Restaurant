Welcome!

We opened our doors in 1996 in the scenic Milford CT. Just a quick walk from the town green and all the other Milford attractions! We offer healthy, vegan, and vegetarian options as well as excellent Happy Hour Deals

Welcome

Location Our Story Contact Stop By!
We are situated right off the Milford, CT Green. Walking distance from the local piano! This beautiful Downtown Milford location allows you to come in for a meal or drinks, then enjoy a walk on the Milford Sound, watch some boats, and try some of the other attractions. We support local businesses! Stop by and ask about some lovely stores this town has to offer.
We are family owned and operated for nearly three decades! We started this restaurant as just a pair of people with a dream. Our three sons now help out in the store and are often better at running the place than we are! Come on in to our friendly atmosphere for great eats at even better prices.
Want to get in contact? You can send us an email, give us a call, or just stop by! We love meeting our customers on a one-to-one basis. Give us some feedback, ask us some questions, or simply come by and say 'hi!' We are looking forward to sharing more of our story with you, and to have you contribute to ours.
Come by with your friends or family for a great atmosphere. We have outdoor seating when the weather is nice, and it provides a great view of the green. People watch, sip on some wine or freshly made juice, and take a deep breath! Enjoy the atmosphere and comforting meals we provide daily.
